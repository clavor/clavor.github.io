Who is Clavor?

Clavor was born on May 27, 1994, in the city of Denver, Colorado. He grew up alongside his two younger siblings, Jonathan and Freddy. The three of them were always getting in trouble and always made a mess around the house. However, when it came to their hobbies, they were all really passionate about them and got so engulfed into them that it seemed as if their entire personalities changed.


At the age of 12, he began to use more than just a piano. With the help of his uncle, they made their own compact studio inside of his uncleâ€™s garage. With this, he began creating music and added a variety of sounds using his synths and other equipment. Finally, on March 1 of 2011, he released his first song named Salt. This song received a lot of positive feedback and, to his surprise, reached number 3 on ITunes.
